About Me

Hi, I’m Kate Heise — a critical care nurse practitioner, data analyst, and systems thinker passionate about solving hard problems in healthcare.

I work at the intersection of clinical care and technology, building tools that make hospital workflows smarter, faster, and more human-centered. Whether it’s automating risk scores in Epic, streamlining ICU rounding, or empowering frontline teams through innovation frameworks like RAPID, I believe the best solutions start with the people doing the work.

I’ve worked in critical care for over a decade, earned an MBA, and completed a data analytics certificate — all while staying grounded in bedside practice. I love making complex systems feel simple, and I’m always looking for ways to bridge the gap between ideas and implementation.

Outside of work, you can find me chasing after my kid, listening to audiobooks on 1.25x speed, or drinking Bubbl’r and writing code after bedtime.

Want to connect or collaborate? Email me or check out my projects and blog.
